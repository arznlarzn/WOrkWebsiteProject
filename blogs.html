So, he think's he's a writer...

At least, he really want's to be.


I think this is less about wanting to 'be' anything, and more about wanting to share what feels important. For all of the people who don't know; the people that don't realize what they really, really are; the people that are hung up; that ask themselves "what am I doing here?"... But this is actually, all of us.

Some things just feel too important not to do something,
and for me, this is just the beginning of the something.

Here's what I write about:



BadBuddhist was my first attempt at creating anything when I first got sober. The plan was: it was to be a blog about Mindfulness In Recovery. Totally sounds excellent and it's two things I absolutely love. To create something that could actually be successful, I realized pretty quickly that I would need to put in a lot more work than I was actually willing to do at the time. Unfortunately, I believe I lost all of the posts I'd originally wrote. However, I would rather seperate these topics. Under BadBuddhist, I am going to write about mindfulness and awareness/conciousness.


I realize that I hold much more knowledge than your average person when it comes to technology, electronics, and how to shop and what to look for in such things. I worked for Best Buy for something like 4 years, but that only gave me entry into the world of technology. I learned a lot while I was there and became some what of a laymen expert, however, I discovered that this was a passion that I had never realized before. So, here I will write about technology.
