ThisIsRecovery



In 2020, I was writing primarily, if not solely, about recovery. There was a bit of 'mindfulness', but I had just entered back into society and into my life from several years of hard, relentless addiction. Recovery is still just as relevant to me now as it was then, however, I have found that I am passionate about more than just my recovery. I want to only write real, meaningful things about recovery and my experiences in it.

And I am not some poser, either. I'm a real, down-bad, bottom-barrel, homeless junkie. Just...
Not anymore.

Thank you for your interest in reading my blog posts. Currently, my first priority is this website and having a solid group of projects for in my portfolio.
