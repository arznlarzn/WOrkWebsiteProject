Here's the TL/DR ::
I was lost in addiction. The bad kind.
In 2020, my mother and father gave me another chance.
I was truly exhausted and had seen enough.
I missed...
Being Alive.
Because I wasn't. And I hadn't been for a long time.
I knew if I continued,
I wouldn't be around for much longer.
And I found my path...

I was in Salt Lake City, UT.
I began working again and that was with Best Buy.
I love electronics, but selling them is not it.
I found myself miserable and decided
I was going to work in recovery.
One month later, I would be hired at Evnsq,
Balance House's Inpatient program.
I would stay working with different, needy populations,
and loving all of them, some
more than others.

Since beginning this journey, I've been practicing meditation.
First, I used headspace for about a year.
It was excellent for teaching me
how "to sit", but
I could feel something was missing.
Then I found the Waking Up app from Sam Harris.
And it has since completely changed my perception of everything.
And at least now, sometimes I can see clearly.

Today, I am in Portland, OR, and I'm glad to be home,
in the Pacific North West..
I am doing what I can to get into the field of Technology.
Whatever it takes.
I am looking to begin school in 2025 on the road towards a Bachelors in Computer Science.
But in the meantime,
I am building.

I will be looking for a mentor.
I am also looking to begin school in January
for Computer Science,
but I'm waiting to make the commitment.
I've already managed most of the prerequesites.
FAFSA. Schools.
I've got it all figured out.
And I'd like to begin sooner than later.
